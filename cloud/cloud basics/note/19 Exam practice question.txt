19 Exam practice question


Question: From AWS
Under the shared responsiblity model, which of the following is the customer responsible for?
a. Ensuring that disk drives are wiped after use
b. Ensuring that firmware is updated on hardware devices
c. Ensuring that data is encrypted at rest
d. Ensuring that network cables are category six or higher


My Answer: C
Reason is that only C is within our control. Everything else are something only the Cloud provider, regardless of the service used, can control or change


Actual Answer Explanation
a. Ensuring that disk drives are wiped after use
	> handled by the cloud provider
b. Ensuring that firmware is updated on hardware devices
	> very much infrastructure related, no way user is responsible for it
c. Ensuring that data is encrypted at rest
	> cloud is not responsible for the data the app generate
d. Ensuring that network cables are category six or higher
	> Infrastructure related stuff, not handled by user
