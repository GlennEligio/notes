25 CapEx vs OpEx


CapEx - Capital Expenditure
 > refers to the capital cost of buying to own a hardware
 > full upfront cost
 > savings over time
 > useful when you can predict the scale 
 > usually the cheapest on consistent utilization
 > means "on-prem data centers" on Cloud context
OpEx - Operation Expenditure
 > refers to the cost of "rent" a harware
 > no upfront costs
 > pay for what you use
 > useful in scale or demand fluctuation
 > can be more expensive in the long run
	- due to address the scale or demand fluctuation
