11 Tour of the Console & Services in AWS


Changing Region
 > to change region, look at the top right corner to scan for the Region dropdown
 > choose the one closest to you to reduce latency
 > this will affect:
	- what services is available
	- where the service will be hosted/created
 > for Global Services like Route 53, the region name will change to Global to signify that the service is indeed Global


AWS Regional Services List
 > https://aws.amazon.com/about-aws/global-infrastructure/regional-product-services/
 > the link above will show you all the services offered by a specific region
