27 RHCSA RHEL 8 - Assign physical volumes to volume groups


Commands
vgs
 > display information about volume groups

vgdisplay
 > similar to vgs
 > display infromation about the volume groups

vgcreate VG_NEW PV
 > used to create a volume group
 > takes two inputs
	VG_NEW	volume group name to create
	PV		physical volume, the disk partition that we created with type LVM
 > ex:
	$ vgcreate vg1 /dev/sdb1


vgextend VG PV ...
 > used to "extend" or add physical volumes to a volume group
 > takes two inputs
	VG	name of existing Volume Group
	PV	physical volumes to add


vgreduce VG PV...
 > remove phyiscal volume(...) from an existing volume group
 > takes two inputs
	VG	name of existing Volume group
	PV	name of the physical volume to remove

vgremove VG
 > removes a Volume group
 > takes one input
	VG 	name of existing volume group
