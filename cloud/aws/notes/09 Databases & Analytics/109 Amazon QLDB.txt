109 Amazon QLDB

Amazon QLDB
 > QLDB stands for 'Quantum Ledger Database'
 > A ledger is a book recording financial transactions
 > Fully Managed, Serverless, High Available, Replication across 3 AZs
 > Used to review history of all the changes made to your application data over time
 > Immutable system, which means no entry can be removed or modified, cryptographically verifiable
 > 2-3x better performance than common ledger blockchain frameworks, can manipulate data using SQL
 > Difference with Amazon Managed BlockChain:
	- QLDB have NO DECENTRALIZATION COMPONENT, in accordance with financial regulation rules
