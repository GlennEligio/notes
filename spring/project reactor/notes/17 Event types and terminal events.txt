17 Event types and terminal events


Mono vs Flux
 > Mono: 0 or 1 time
 > Flux: 0 or N times

What Mono or Flux emits
1. An item
2. A complete event
3. A failure event


NOTES:
1. When a terminal event (complete or failure) is emitted, the Flux or Mono will NOT emit anything afterward (item or another event)
